Geetha Govindam (2018) is a highly successful Indian Telugu-language romantic comedy directed by Parasuram, starring Vijay Devarakonda and Rashmika Mandanna. The plot follows an innocent young lecturer, Govind, who is mistaken for a pervert by Geetha, a woman who later turns out to be his sister-in-law's sister, as they fall in love despite initial misunderstandings

Hi Nanna- Father Emotional Movie

Hi Nanna is a heartwarming Telugu family drama about a single father, Viraj, and his six-year-old daughter, Mahi, who has cystic fibrosis, focusing on their deep bond and Mahi's quest to learn about her absent mother, leading to a chance encounter with a woman who holds the key to their past. The movie explores themes of love, memory, loss, and resilience as Viraj navigates fatherhood while hiding painful truths from Mahi, unraveling a touching story of connection and destiny.
